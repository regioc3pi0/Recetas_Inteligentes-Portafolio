# ✨ Features — Smart Recipes: Cost Manager

---

## 🍽️ Recipe Management

### Recipe Creation

Build complete recipes with name, description, category, prep time, and serving count.
Each recipe is a structured card that groups all your dish's data in one place.

### Ingredients with Real Costs

Add ingredients to each recipe specifying quantity, unit of measure, and actual
purchase price. The app automatically calculates how much that ingredient costs
within the recipe based on the proportion used.

### Automatic Cost Per Serving

Once a recipe is complete, the app shows the total cost and cost per serving
calculated in real time. Change any price or quantity and the result updates instantly.

---

## 💰 Cost Management

### Ingredient Inventory

Register your ingredients with up-to-date prices. When a supply price changes
in the market, update it once and the cost of every recipe using it recalculates
automatically.

### Suggested Sale Price

Based on the calculated cost, the app can suggest a sale price by applying your
desired profit margin. Ideal for food businesses that need to set fair, profitable prices.

### Cost History

Keep a record of how your recipe costs have changed over time. Useful for detecting
ingredient inflation and adjusting sale prices accordingly.

---

## 📄 Export & Sharing

### PDF Export

Generate a professional PDF of any recipe with all its details: ingredients,
quantities, cost per serving, and suggested sale price. Ready to print or share digitally.

### Share Recipes

Share your recipes directly from the app via WhatsApp, email, notes, or any
compatible app on your iPhone.

## 🗃️ Organization

### Customizable Categories

Organize your recipes by category — breakfasts, main dishes, desserts, drinks,
or whatever you define. Find any recipe in seconds.

### Quick Search

Search by recipe name or ingredient to find exactly what you need without
browsing the entire catalog.
