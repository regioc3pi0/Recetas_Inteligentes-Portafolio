Do you actually know how much each dish you prepare costs?
Smart Recipes is a cooking app with built-in cost management, designed for home cooks,
food business entrepreneurs, and chefs who need to know the exact cost of every recipe
before selling or serving it.

Build your recipes, add your ingredients with real prices, and let the app automatically
calculate the cost per serving. No spreadsheets. No guesswork.
